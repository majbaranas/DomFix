### Current Structure (After Critical Fixes)
```

  ├─ uid
  ├─ email
  ├─ role
  ├─ onboardingCompleted
  └─ createdAt

  ├─ lat
  ├─ lng
  └─ updatedAt                    ← NO "online" field!
```
